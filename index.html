INFORMATION SECURITY FOR THE REST OF US

We have been forced into a world where we have no choice but to use computers and the internet for almost every aspect of our lives. Unfortunately the people who are smart enough to make this all work have not taken the time to explain it to those who do not have thousands of hours to figure it out. This is a small attempt at bridging that gap, just a little.
